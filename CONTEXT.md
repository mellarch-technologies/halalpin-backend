## Launch Cities Profile

### Hyderabad (Primary Market)
- **Muslim Population**: 2,107,047 (30.13% of 7M total)
- **Strategy**: Anchor city, primary focus, 1,000-1,200 vendors
- **Target Areas**: Old City, Tolichowki, Chandrayangutta, Mehdipatnam
- **Launch**: Public (Month 4)

### Lucknow (Secondary Market)
- **Muslim Population**: ~800,000 (25-30% of 3.2M total)
- **Strategy**: Tier 2 validation, North India coverage, 500-600 vendors
- **Target Areas**: Chowk, Aminabad, Hazratganj, Gomti Nagar
- **Launch**: Public (Month 4)

### Chennai (Stealth Beta)
- **Muslim Population**: 439,270 (9.45% of 4.6M total)
- **Strategy**: Founder's home base, friends/family testing, UX lab, 200-300 vendors
- **Target Areas**: Triplicane, Royapettah, Thousand Lights, Anna Nagar
- **Launch**: Stealth (Month 4), Public (Month 7+ if primary cities succeed)
